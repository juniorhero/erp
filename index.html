Akkuba ERP

Akkuba Enterprise Resource Planner (ERP) is used in the management of enterprise for the computerization of enterprise processes such as management of customer data, employee data, client data, financial data, sales reports, attendance reports, inventory details, equity details and payroll details. An ERP can handle different user roles such as manager, CEO, employees, customers and has abstraction features for its users. It is the core software used by all enterprises as it provides an interface for the overall management of the enterprise. Akkuba ERP System is easy to use, easily configurable as well as economical in terms of time and cost. Moreover, it can adapt easily to any browser or device through its inbuilt bootstrap framework.
